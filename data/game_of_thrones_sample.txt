BOOK: A Game of Thrones

CHAPTER: EDDARD I
The winter was coming. Eddard Stark rode south with his men, leaving Winterfell behind. The cold wind howled through the trees, a constant reminder of the words of House Stark. King Robert Baratheon was arriving with the royal procession. Eddard wondered if this was the last time he would see the walls of his ancestral home. He remembered his sister Lyanna and the promise he made her on her deathbed.

CHAPTER: JON I
Jon Snow stood near the back of the great hall, watching the royal family. He was a bastard, and bastards did not sit at the high table. Uncle Benjen Stark was talking to him about the Night's Watch. The Wall was a cold and lonely place, but Jon felt he had no other home. He wanted to take the black and guard the realms of men.

CHAPTER: DAENERYS I
Daenerys Targaryen looked out at the narrow sea. Her brother Viserys told her they would return home soon. The dragon was asleep, but he would wake when the time was right. Khal Drogo was coming to see her, a powerful warlord of the Dothraki. Viserys would trade her to the Dothraki horse lord in exchange for a crown.
